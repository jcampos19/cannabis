What is Bud Bargains?

Bud Bargains is the premiere site for finding the best local deals on bud! We are community oriented to connect our members to local dispensaries with exclusive deals and specials!

How It Works

  1. Local retailers provide our members with daily bargains and specials.
  2. Our members vote on the quality of a bargain. The highest voted bargain at any given moment will be displayed on the front page.
  3. Sold out bargains are removed from the bargain stream to ensure freshness.
  4. Sign up for a free, anonymous account to rate retailers and follow your favorite retailers!
  5. SMS Now allows you to connect to a retailer with one click!

I'm a Retailer, How Do I Get Started?

  1. Create a free acount.
  2. Add credits. Credits allow you to publish bargains to the Bargain Stream.
  3. Post Bargains! You can easily post bargains from your account dashboard.

Once you've posted a bargain, you can analyze it's performance by tracking customer engagement in real time in an easy to use Dashboard! You can also manage your account and access other useful features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Creating the Bargains?

The dispensaries are creating the bargains. They will be providing daily deals which can also be renewed (reposted) daily.

How Long are Bargains Available?

The bargains are set to expire 24 hours from the posting time and date. If the bargain is sold out, the dispensaries will mark the bargains as sold out. Sold out bargains are displayed with a large SOLD OUT across their display.

What is an Uptoke?

An uptoke is a way for each member to express that they are pleased with or like the bargain. By clicking on the thumbs up icon, members can give a single “vote” or “like” to that particular bargain.

What bargains are popular?

Bargains will be defaulted to sorting by uptokes. The more uptokes a bargain has, it usually means the more popular the bargain is.

What do the Filters and Sorts mean?

The Distance Filter is a way to only show bargains from dispensaries that are located within the selected number of miles from your zipcode.
